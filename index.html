THE INSTRUCTOR CHALLENGE

You see the start. You see the end. Not what happens in between.

Between assignment and submission, students struggle, search for help, and revise their thinking.

That process stays invisible. You only get the final answer.

The instructor challenge is seeing and supporting the learning that happens in between.

  • Self explanation
