The problem

AI is moving faster than classroom systems can adapt.

01

AI is completing homework instead of students.

Students can get final answers without showing the thinking that learning requires.

02

Professors have no visibility into real understanding.

Instructors see submitted work, but not the moments where students get stuck.

03

Universities are being forced to ban AI.

Bans are difficult to enforce and can remove useful support from honest students.

The solution

Controlled AI tutoring for real learning.

MyTA gives students guided AI help without direct answer generation. It keeps the interaction focused on reasoning, course context, and the next useful step.

For instructors, MyTA turns learning activity into visibility: where students struggle, which concepts need attention, and how AI is being used inside the course workflow.
